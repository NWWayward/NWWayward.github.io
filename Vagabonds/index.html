Clanless start as Vagabonds, as do the following three clans.

Clans from other organizations can opt to become Vagabonds (instead of being part of their original faction); if they were from a noble clan, they are stripped of their noble title and rights to do so.

Chinoike

The Chinoike bloodline, known for its chilling Dojutsu and being Genjutsu specialists. During a brutal civil war in the Land of Steam’s Valley of Hell, they were almost wiped out; not by conventional forces, but by relentless persecution from ‘Jashinists’ (worshipping Jashin, the god of pain, death, and destruction) who sought to exterminate them to steal or profane their power. Facing extinction, the Chinoike descended into a ‘defensive insanity’, adopting the rituals of their tormentors. They formed desperate blood cults and embraced a twisted, violent form of Jashinism themselves as they unlocked more of their Doujutsu. Their clan name has changed over the years by their host village in an attempt to conceal their bloodline; since their eyes are still considered a powerful tool for Genjutsu users or as trophies of war. Given their teachings, they are often considered “Executioners” by those who employ them..

Chinoike begin with 1 less unique point.

Chinoike begin with Elemental Affinity: Ketsuton, which includes Suiton, and begin with the Genjutsu Training ability, for free.

-=[ Ketsuryūgan - Blood Dragon Eye ]=-

Ketsuryūgan is a doujutsu, or ‘eye technique’; when activated, the user’s Sclera glows a blood red; their pupils often go black as a result and their iris change to a horizontally rectangular shape; glowing the same kind of blood red as the Sclera. Survivors record the witness of the Doujutsu feels unnervingly penetrative or chilling - not in the same way Hyuuga can see through walls, but more like your soul is being judged.

Activating Ketsuryūgan is a Speed 0 Action with an Upkeep of 2. With the Preparation ability, it can be activated when you roll initiative.

While active, you gain a bonus of X to Awareness and Survival rolls, and reduce visibility penalties by X/2 (rounding down) against those with a Bleed status, with X being their highest severity Bleed status. Whenever someone reduces their Bleed status (typically by the natural over-time reduction) in combat with you (but not when you do so yourself), you gain a number of HP equal to the reduction in their Bleed status. (Bleed being lowered from 5 to 2 would grant you 3 HP)

Additionally with this Doujutsu active, you can differentiate between people and clones (except for Kage Bunshin) by the way the iron in the blood gives them away.

Finally, while Ketsuryūgan is active; it unlocks the user’s ability to convert Suiton and Genjutsu into Ketsuton variations.

-=[ Ketsuton - Blood Release ]=-

The ability to use Ketsuton (Blood Release) is a developed trait that grants the user mastery over iron-rich fluids - most notably blood. At the most basic level, Ketsuton allows the user mix the water element with small razors of metal-rich blood to further wound and drain a target of their precious lifeline.

Any Suiton or Genjutsu technique can be converted to Ketsuton as a Chakra +5, Speed +0 link, with the following changes:

  • Suiton inflict a Bleed equal to their Rank if they deal any damage after all reductions; they gain +2 * wound severity (Minor = 1, Major = 2, etc) extra if they wound.
  • Suiton which inflict Bleed from the above ability can optionally have its total Bleed halved, in order to add it to an existing Ketsuton Bleed source, which is considered a single source per Chinoike. Bleed stacked in this manner has a maximum of CHA/5, and only one source can be stacked; all others are considered separate sources.
  • Suiton which create or add water to the battlefield can have additional gallons made (of “blood”), by paying 1 HP per “Blood Point”, to a maximum of double its normal amount; while it is actually more of a mixture of water and ichor, it looks grotesquely the latter.
  • “Blood Points” on the battlefield can not be removed or accessed by anyone except a Chinoike with an active Ketsuryugan, and can be used in place of Gallons of Water.
  • Genjutsu converted with Ketsuton can be used without handseals, so long as the target(s) of the Genjutsu has a (Rank * 2) Bleed effect on them; thus, a D-rank genjutsu could be used without handseals on someone with a Bleed of 4, while a C-rank would require a Bleed of 6. If affecting multiple people, all of them need to have a high enough Bleed status to qualify, else you must perform handseals.
  • Minor wounds from Ketsuton do not cause Genjutsu to break so long as the target has a Bleed status of (Rank * 4) or higher, based on the Genjutsu in question (An E-rank can be maintained with a Bleed status of 5 or more, while a D-rank requires 8 or more, etc).
  • Ketsuton retains Suiton’s normal elemental strengths and weaknesses.

–( Haiketsushoushuen - Blood Poisoning Banquet )–

A favorite of the Jashinists, fledgling practitioners would often intentionally drink agonizing and debilitating poisons before combat just for the chance to share the sensation with others. By focusing their tainted blood into a thin lance to penetrate the skin, they would inject their favored toxins into friend and foe alike. Ketsuton Ninjutsu, Rank D

  • Chakra: 16
  • Damage: 5d10+30
  • Range: 15
  • Speed: 12
  • Seal Speed: 8
  • Tags: Piercing, Projectile
  • Requires: Ketsuryūgan Active

Effect: As part of casting Haiketsushoshuen, you must sacrifice X HP, to a maximum of (Cha/5), which does not add SP. Haiketsushoushuen’s damage for wounding purposes is increased by (X)%, and any poison(s) you are affected with have their remaining duration reduced by X.

If this technique wounds its target, and you are currently suffering from a poison, the target makes a Status roll against the original Toxicology roll of the poison you are afflicted with, with a penalty to their Resistance equal to the severity of the wound (-1 for Minor, -2 for Major, etc). If they fail, they are afflicted with the same poison, with a duration of (10 + X), or the poison’s remaining duration (for you), whichever is lower.

–( Senkou Kisuu - Scarlet Kiss )–

The technique that causes most to fear meeting a Chinoike’s eyes, as all they must do is stare at someone who is already bleeding. To the victim, they swear they see the Chinoike’s horizontal pupils change, dilating and pulsating as they become weaker, inevitably leaving them vulnerable to a Chinoike’s mental manipulations.

  • Ketsuton Ninjutsu, Rank C
  • Chakra: 20
  • Accuracy: +X/5
  • Range: 20
  • Speed: 15
  • Requires: Ketsuryūgan Active, target with a Bleed status

Effect: If this hits, make a status roll. Your target’s highest bleed status is removed if they pass a status roll, or halved (rounded up) if they fail. Either way, they take damage equal to X * (NDB or 5, whichever is higher), where X is the removed bleed’s original severity. This damage ignores Damage Reduction. If this would wound your target, it does not do so; instead of wounding, you inflict a -X/5 penalty to Stamina, Chakra Exhaustion, and to their next Genjutsu Defense roll.

You recover HP (not Vitality) equal to X * 2, up to your maximum HP - any in excess of your maximum HP is instead added to the battlefield as Blood Points.

–( Hijutsu: Ketsuseitou Ryōiki — Secret Technique: Blood Subjugation Domain )–

Commonly believed to be the ultimate expression of Ketsuton, the Blood Subjugation Domain demonstrates an absolute mastery over blood and iron. By emanating an intense aura of control, a masterful Ketsuton practitioner expresses an almost magnetic pull that draws the blood weeping from the wounds of friend and foe alike, then turns that traitorous blood against its very source.

  • Ketsuton Ninjutsu, Rank B
  • Chakra: 30
  • Upkeep: 6
  • Speed: 14
  • Area: 10 + Blood Points on field
  • Tags: Point-Blank Area of Effect
  • Requires: Ketsuryūgan Active

Effect: Anytime a Bleed status is reduced within this technique’s Area, Blood Points are added to the battlefield equal to the amount it is reduced. This has no maximum, and does increase its own Area accordingly.

Anyone within this technique’s area, except the user, gains a Bleed status automatically of (your RES/20). This bleed may not be reduced while in the area.

While within the technique’s Area, Ketsuryugan and Ketsuton Genjutsu considers targets based off of their highest Bleed status attained while they were in this jutsu’s area (rather than their current highest Bleed status). If someone leaves and re-enters this jutsu’s area, then this effect is reset to their new highest Bleed, even if it was lower than before.

Souma no Ko

This “clan” is not a true clan, but the name of a life altering medical jutsu that can only be performed on twins. According to the medical journals, exclusively those funded by the military, the first use of Souma no Ko was a life saving medical ninjutsu performed for altruistic reasons. A pair of children, twins, their names lost to time, were mortally wounded in a raid on a certain village in Rice Country. In an act of desperation, a medical genius of a shinobi jury rigged a technique to convert both twins into a biological life support system for the other, saving both of their lives. Critics cite the lack of detail (what doctor? What twins? What village? How did they conveniently have injuries that complimented each other like that?) To dispute this altruistic and benign origin story. Regardless of the technique’s true origins, it is known that the first patients become shinobi, possibly out of gratitude for being saved.

Those patients started to adapt a fighting style using their unique physiology, the military found this to be tactically advantageous, the technique leaked to the larger shinobi community, and the rest is history. Twins started having the technique used on them simply for the tactical benefits, rather than any medical necessity. The medical corp pulled from actually ill children who benefited, twins who were in the system for any reason, and twins whose parents accepted a payout for allowing their children to be experimented on and promising a career as a shinobi. While the technique is surprisingly safe to use, because the only qualification was “twins,” a lot of the results were either bad shinobi, or resented being permanently fused with their sibling.

It was not the outcry of the patients that ultimately saw Souma no Ko’s decline in use by the various nations. For unknown reasons, Souma no Kou simply kills the patient when used on someone with a Kekkai Genkai. Several prominent families lost not one but two children as a cost to learn that fact. Out of spite, those families went on to lobby for Souma no Kou to be banned, and as part of that campaign they lended power to the voice of dissatisfied patients. These days Souma no Ko is only very rarely used, and it exclusively (legally) used it on twins who medically benefit. Research has gone into making Souma no Ko safer to use on older sets of twins, and a military career is no longer required (although if you don’t become a shinobi, your family must pay for the procedure or you must commit to working at an approved civilian job for a period of time).

-=( Multi-Connecting Strike )=-

The twins inhabit the same physical space. At any given time one of the twins is the “main” twin. The other twin can project their body parts out of any part of the main twin’s body. Which twin is the main twin can change. The second twin’s head, however, is always visible, though it can be easily hidden under clothes, et cetera… if the twin is cooperating that day.

  • Type: Ability
  • Cost: 20
  • Limit: 1

Effect: You are two bodies–two people–in one. You may have either of them talk, and they each control their own set of limbs. One twin can project their limbs from any part of the other twin’s body.

Mechanically speaking, they do not count as separate characters or targets except as outlined in this clan’s abilities and jutsu.

You may ignore one wound per location, as the twins compensate for each other’s physical failings, and change which wounds are ignored whenever you are wounded. You may not ignore wounds caused by using the Eight Gates. Having two heads makes it more difficult to affect the sum with Genjutsu, resulting in a +2 bonus to Genjutsu Defense against single target Genjutsu. Finally, you have four hands with which to hold things and perform jutsu. Because of your extra hands, you may perform handseals as though two fewer of your arms were in an arm lock (minimum 0). While one or more of those hands are holding a weapon, you may not take bonus actions (described below) or use Multi-Connecting Whirlwind.

On your turn in initiative, when you take a non-Variable Speed action, you may perform a Basic Unarmed Arrack, Unarmed Jutsu, or a non-finisher Combo Taijutsu as a simultaneous “bonus action.” To do so, you pay the normal costs for your actions plus an additional X/4 AP, rounded up. X is the final combined Speed of both your regular action and the bonus action provided by this ability. You only gain Combo Counter from one of your attacks, but do not lose your Combo for performing a Non Combo action as long as one of your actions continues the Combo. If at least one of your attacks hits, you are not considered to have missed for buffs such as Competitive or Relentless. Your IC advances by the slower of the two actions, which also determines how far you can move as part of the attack. Your opponent’s IC advances only be the slowest of their defenses used against these simultaneous actions. When you use this option, you lose the passive advantages of this ability until you can next perform a non interrupt action. Clones may never make this bonus action.

-=( Separation )=-

What was once made whole, you tear asunder. The twins separate themselves into two distinct bodies. They chakra literally hold the bodies together, compensating for the lack of the other twin… for a time.

  • Type: Ninjutsu, D Rank
  • Chakra: 12
  • Speed: 10
  • Tags: Clone

Effect: You split your twin off from your main body (who is the main body is not mechanically relevant, and can change from use to use). Mechanically, your twin functions as a single Clone, except their attacks are not automatically Held Back, they match your Fatigue Level instead of having their own, and they do not cause Clone Miss Chance. Upkeeps with the Preparation tag that are active when this jutsu is used affect both the clone and main body, without needing to increase their Upkeep. Any Gates open or stored Natural Chakra Points are likewise shared, but the twins pull from the same pool of Gate AP and NC. Equipment is not duplicated, but as part of using this jutsu you can decide whether the Main Body or Clone has which piece of equipment.

The Clone may perform any action. Non Interrupt actions cost (Action’s Final Speed)/2 AP, rounded up. You make any Fatigue rolls for the clone. Any damage dealt to the Clone is subtracted from your Vitality or HP, as appropriate.

Recombining is a Speed 5 action that may be performed by either your main body or the clone, so long as they are Range of Touch from one another, and neither one is Grappling someone. Recombining ends this jutsu. So long as they are within range, nothing except being Stunned or Dead can prevent the twins from taking this action. Even unconscious or defeated twins can recombine. When the twins recombine, any wounds or status effects that were gained after splitting from either twin apply to the now recombined twin.

Twins that have been separated for too long begin to die. They may not heal or recover at all while separated. Every fifteen minutes they remain separated, they advance one fatigue level. If they hit Fatigue Level 10, they die.

While separated, the twins lose all benefits of Multi-Connecting Strike.

-=( Multi-Connecting Whirlwind )=-

The twins use their four legs to kick off the ground repeatedly and spin at high speeds, with a force strong enough to generate a whirlwind.

  • Type: Combo Taijutsu, C Rank
  • Stamina: 25
  • Damage: Basic Unarmed Strike
  • Speed: 20
  • Area: 2
  • Special: Finisher
  • Combo Bonus: +2d* Damage, +0 to +1 Area (user’s choice).
  • Requires: Can not be Separated

Effect: Multi-Connecting Whirlwind has only a single Accuracy, and is treated as one attack. However, if the opponent uses a defense where they roll for a result (such as dodge or parry), they must roll twice.

If their first defensive roll fails, that roll is used to determine how much, if any, Partial Success applies to the attack’s damage. If the second roll also fails (with partial success not applying to its result), your attack’s damage dice are tripled (from, for example, 5d4 to 5d4*3).

If their first defensive roll succeeds, they still make a second defensive roll. If they succeed a second time, they have successfully avoided the attack; if they fail, then this technique deals its damage normally.

If someone blocks against this technique, it does not get its damage dice tripled. If someone elects to not defend against this technique, however, it does.

When you use this jutsu, you lose the benefits of Multi-Connecting Strike until you can next perform a non interrupt action. You may not use a Bonus Action on the same IC you use this Jutsu.

-=( Kisei Kikai no Jutsu - Demon Parasite Technique )=-

Using their chakra, a twin disassembles their body on a cellular and proteinic level so that they can enter a target’s body. They then start reforming themselves, taking over certain parts of the opponent’s body and gradually corroding the parts they haven’t entered yet. Ultimately, the target dies, achieving this technique’s primary purpose: assassination.

  • Type: Medical Ninjutsu, B Rank
  • Chakra: 20
  • Stamina: 20
  • Speed: 16
  • Delay: 5
  • Upkeep: 10
  • Requires: Must be Separated, and can only be used by the “clone,” which must be grappling the target.
  • Special: Souma no Ko patients may learn this jutsu if they qualify for B Rank General Ninjutsu or B Rank Grapple Taijutsu, ignoring the usual talent requirement for Medical Ninjutsu.

Effect: This Jutsu hits automatically as though it were Grapple Taijutsu. If the target breaks free of the Grapple before then, then this jutsu is Aborted and the user only pays a 10 Chakra, 10 Stamina cost instead.

When this jutsu resolves, the twin that used this jutsu fuses their body and the target’s. The Grapple may not be ended until this jutsu ends, even by effects that render the other person immune to grapples. However, the defender can use Shift Grapple to gain control of the grapple normally.

Every time the fused twin uses a Grapple Jutsu or attacks the target, the target takes (Action’s Speed)xCHA/10 irreducible damage, which can not wound. Actions which would normally end the grapple without ending this jutsu may not be taken.

While fused, when the twin or the target take damage or suffer a wound, the other suffers from that damage and/or effect, which may not be reduced or negated. The transferred damage does not, itself, inflict additional wounds. Damage and wounds caused by this jutsu are not transferred.

Suzuri

Long ago there was an artist who was captivated by the world. He would travel and paint everything he could see with his eyes. His paintings were so vivid, that it almost as though they could come to life. One day, a small creature, a cricket with the power to paint, saw the boy’s art and made him a wager. If he could capture his heart’s desire in a painting, the cricket would grant it, but if the painting was deemed in poor taste, he would be cursed. The boy thought for a long time what he wanted to paint, what his true heart’s desire was. He spent days, starting, stopping, throwing away drafts of paintings he would start in fear that it wasn’t enough.

The boy finally gave the cricket a blank canvas. The cricket blinked a couple of times. “What is this? Have you given up.”

The boy responds, “You asked me to draw my heart’s desire, but my desire is to live, and to record those experiences for others who will never get the pleasure to see what I’ve seen. There is no single thing that I could paint for you, so the blank canvas can capture my heart’s desire.”

The cricket smiles. “You will see your heart’s desire come to life, through your chakra, to your art. This I bestow upon you, as my pupil, and you will pass this gift onto your children, and their children, and their children’s children, so long as their desire is to share what they draw unto others.”

The Cricket’s name was Issun, and his gift was bestowed in the original bearer of the Suzuri bloodline.

100 years later, the Clan is mostly made up of travelers who rarely meet one another unless in passing. Sometimes there are reunions, but this separation and secrecy has kept them from being found and exploited. Through Ink, Paint, or Charcoal, there are a variety of variants for this jutsu to manifest. As all artists have a different style.

-=[ Chakra-Infused Ink ]=-

By infusing their chakra into the ink beforehand and drawing objects with a brush on a scroll (something they can do very quickly), members of this clan are able to bring their creations to life by animating the ink directly off the page. These creations grow to life-size once they have been brought to life.

  • Type: Ability
  • Cost: 0

Effects: Instead of utilizing Handseals to form chakra, you utilize a blank scroll and a physical medium (ink, charcoal, paints, etc). While the medium is easy to acquire in large quantities, blank scrolls must be utilized for every jutsu that ordinarily has a Seal Speed with it (even if you would reduce that Seal Speed to 0), though if you can avoid the need for handseals at all, you similarly bypass the requirement of a blank scroll.

In exchange, you change all Seal Speeds to “Draw Speed”, which follows Seal Speed’s normal rules except that your Draw Speed action can not be interrupted by damage. However, you can never benefit from the Single-Handed Seals unique’s ability to utilize only one hand to draw (one is always needed to support the scroll you’re drawing on). On the other hand, because you do not require your hands to be linked together at any point, a Uchiha could not copy your techniques (due to the lack of handseals), nor could an arm lock slow or prevent you from drawing. Other increases or reductions to Seal Speeds (such as some Wounds, or Seal Master) apply to your Draw Speed similarly.

-=[ Chōjū Giga - Super Beast Imitation Art ]=-

The Super Beast Imitating Drawing is a technique that allows the user to animate ink drawings, which act upon the user’s will.

  • Type: Ability
  • Cost: 30
  • Limit: 1
  • Requires: Chakra-Infused Ink

Effects: When you perform a non-Clone Genjutsu, Ninjutsu, or non-Weapon Taijitsu one rank lower than your maximum (up to B Rank, when you can use A Ranks) that does not have an Upkeep you may, as a +1 Seal Speed Link, use Chakra-Infused Ink and create a drawing of an Ink Beast using the technique instead. The Ink Beast comes to life, leaps from the scroll next to you, and may act on the next IC. Jutsu that require talents to use (i.e. Medical, Sealing, Summoning) may not be used this way.

The Ink Beast uses your statistics, abilities, uniques, enhancement talents, rank benefits, and skills, ignoring temporary modifiers (except Fatigue, as described below, and your current number of sixth sense ranks) but it has +5 dodge and Stealth, and is destroyed if it is successfully hit by a damaging attack. It is immune to status effects except Visibility Penalties, as the ink is either unaffected by or flows around any obstacle. It has penalties based on the higher of Fatigue Level 1 or your current Fatigue Level if you are present.

The Ink Beast may only take the Move, Dodge, or Hide actions. They may also or perform the jutsu used to make it. It may hide by disguising itself as being part of a drawing or writing. It has a simple intelligence, but will follow basic instructions (i.e. “Hide in this scroll and use your jutsu on the first person to open it other than me.”)

When the Ink Beast performs its jutsu, it ignores any costs and seal speed. The Jutsu is used with the same X value and choices as when the Ink Beast was created. The Jutsu is used at its Base Speed. AP and WP may not be spent on the Ink Beast’s behalf to modify the Jutsu. Abilities which increase the Jutsu’s Chakra and/or Stamina cost may not be used. After performing the Jutsu, the Ink Beast is destroyed.

Ink Beasts who are not Hidden on a surface that can contain them are destroyed at the end of combat (or scene, out of combat). Ink Beasts that last beyond that limit by being properly hidden are anchored in place. If they move or are moved more than 5 yards from where they are anchored by any means, they are destroyed. Up to 1+(Chakra Control Ranks + Research Ranks)/20 Ink Beasts can be anchored at a time. If Ink Beasts beyond this limit are anchored, the oldest Ink Beast(s) are destroyed until there are no more than 1+(Chakra Control Ranks + Research Ranks)/20 Ink Beasts left. Anchored Ink Beasts are destroyed after 24 hours.

By touching an Ink Beast as a Speed 5 action, the user may destroy it and “download” its memories.

-=[ Chōshin Giga - Super God Imitation Art ]=-

Often employed in one-to-one combat, the user is able to bring forth massive and powerful gods. Boasting greater power than the Super Beast Imitating Drawing, each creation can quickly deliver deadly blows capable of easily destroying stone and the terrain. Because these drawings draw on the emotions of the user, the angrier they become, the more powerful the drawings become.

  • Type: Ability
  • Cost: 30
  • Limit: 1
  • Requires; Super Beast Imitation Art, 20 Chakra Control. 25 Research

Effects: When creating an Ink Beast without a cost other than Chakra, Stamina, and Water, you may increase any Chakra and Stamina Costs the Jutsu has, as well as its Seal Speed, by 5. If you do, create an Ink God instead. Clones may not make Ink Gods.

It follows the rules for an Ink Beast, except it lacks an Ink Beast’s bonuses to Dodge and Stealth, and may not Hide. However, it is not destroyed when it uses its stored jutsu. You may only have up to 2 Ink Gods active at the same time.

-=[ Sumi Bunshin no Jutsu - Ink Clone ]=-

A technique that infuses chakra into ink to form clones of the user. If struck, the ink clones revert back to plain ink.

  • Type: Ninjutsu, C-Rank
  • Chakra: 10 + 3 Per Clone
  • Speed: 7 + 3 Per Clone
  • Drawing Speed: 6 + 2 Per Clone
  • Tags: Clone
  • Requires: Chakra-Infused Ink

Effects: You make up to three clones. These clones may dodge, move independently, and hide. Out of combat, they may perform skill checks. They are immune to status effects except visibility penalties, and flow through cracks ink could realistically seep through. These clones can telepathically communicate with the user. Out of combat, they last for up to an hour.
